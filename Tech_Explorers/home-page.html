What is Tech Explorers?

Tech Explorers is a nonprofit organization centered around STEM-related fields such as engineering and computer science. This organization was founded by students with experience in these fields and who are aiming to spread their understanding and joy of learning in STEM to others outside of the standard classroom. Our members are part of the Engineering Academy and Computer Science Academy at our school, and we hope to make STEM education accessible, fun, and engaging.


Our mission is to empower students through hands-on education that builds their curiosity and confidence in these real-world applications. Tech Explorers provides interactive classes in coding and electronics to introduce students to the foundations of many of the technologies we see in the world today. We focus on creating a supportive learning environment, encouraging students to explore, experience, and experiment as they develop new skills and capabilities.

Our Goals

To teach others about the importance of technology in our lives

To educate the younger generation on how certain technologies work

To offer both online and in-person classes surrounded around STEM topics

To make learning about technology fun and engaging

To share our passion of STEM with the world
