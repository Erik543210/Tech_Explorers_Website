As students at the Bergen County Academies, we take a hands-on, research-driven approach to learning that allows us to explore our interests in fields like engineering and computer science. Through specialized academies, collaborative projects, and access to advanced resources, we gain real-world experience by designing, building, and problem-solving in meaningful ways. Whether it's developing software, working with cutting-edge technology, or conducting independent research, we are constantly challenged to think critically and apply our knowledge beyond the classroom. With this experience, our new goal is to pass it on to the next generation of students who are willing to challenge themselves and twist their brains!
